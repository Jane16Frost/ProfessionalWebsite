Goretober 2020

Goretober is an internet wide art challenge taking place in October. The goal is to make a new art piece every day with the help of a prompt list.
For the 2020 challenge I dedicated a lot of time to each piece, allowing me to push my limits and make incredible pieces each day. All the pieces were completed on time with full color and shading. I had so much fun with this challenge and it brought my digital art skills to the next level.
